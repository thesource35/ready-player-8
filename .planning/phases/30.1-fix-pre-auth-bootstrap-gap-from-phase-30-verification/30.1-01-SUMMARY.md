## Decision Recap

The sheet view + helpers live in `ContentView.swift` next to `AuthGateView`,
NOT in a separate file. Rationale:

- **CLAUDE.md monolithic-file constraint:** "Don't break apart monolithic files
  — fix bugs in place."
- **Tight coupling:** the sheet only exists to be presented from `AuthGateView`;
  no other consumer.
- **Phase 29.1 precedent:** the auth-gate changes followed the same in-place
  pattern.

Per `<planning_context>` "state your decision in the plan", this is recorded
here so future readers don't re-litigate it.
