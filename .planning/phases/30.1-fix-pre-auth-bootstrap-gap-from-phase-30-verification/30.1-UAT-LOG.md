**Deviations from Expected:** Two surfacing-of-real-bugs along the way that are NOT scoped to Phase 30.1 but worth backlog entries (see Hand-off Notes below):
  1. `validateBaseURL` accepts URLs with paths beyond the host (e.g., `https://...supabase.co/rest/v1/`), causing signup to construct `<base>//auth/v1/signup` and hit PostgREST instead of Supabase Auth (PGRST125 "Invalid path").   2. Once `isConfigured == true`, the Configure Backend affordance is replaced by a non-tappable green checkmark — so a user who saved bad credentials that passed `validateBaseURL` but fail at runtime is stuck (Integration Hub is still behind auth). Make the green checkmark tappable to re-open the sheet for editing, OR keep the cyan button visible at low opacity / as a small "Edit" link.
