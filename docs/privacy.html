Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

ConstructionOS ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the ConstructionOS mobile application and website (the "Platform").

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Platform
  • Create and manage your account
  • Suggest connections based on trade, location, and company
  • Process subscription payments through Apple
  • Verify contractor licenses and certifications
  • Send notifications for inspections, bid deadlines, and account activity
  • Generate anonymized market intelligence reports
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store credit card numbers (Apple handles all payments)
  • We do not store passwords in plaintext (all passwords are hashed)
  • We do not track precise GPS location without your permission
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Other Users: Your public profile (name, company, trade, bio, certifications) is visible to other Platform users
  • Service Providers: Supabase (database), Anthropic (AI), Apple (payments) — bound by their own privacy policies
  • Verification Partners: State licensing boards and certification authorities to verify credentials
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or government request
  • Aggregated Data: Anonymized, aggregated data may be used in market intelligence reports (no personally identifiable information)

5. Data Security

  • API keys stored in iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted)
  • All network communications over HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords hashed and salted
  • Two-factor authentication available
  • Face ID / Touch ID biometric lock option
  • Supabase Row Level Security on all database tables
  • Regular security reviews

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time through the Settings tab. Upon deletion:

  • Profile information is removed within 30 days
  • Posts and content are removed from the feed
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained
  • Data required by law may be retained as necessary

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics tracking in Settings
  • Restrict: Limit how we process your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at donovanfagan1991@icloud.com.

8. Children's Privacy

ConstructionOS is designed for construction professionals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If we discover we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

9. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

10. European Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing your data includes:

  • Consent: For optional features like location and notifications
  • Contract: To provide the Platform services you subscribed to
  • Legitimate Interest: For analytics, security, and product improvement

11. Cookies and Tracking

The ConstructionOS mobile app does not use cookies. Our website may use essential cookies for functionality. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the Platform. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
