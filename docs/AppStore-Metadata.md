## Description (4000 chars max)
ConstructionOS is the all-in-one construction operating system built for superintendents, project managers, and executives who refuse to lose control of their jobsite.

COMMAND CENTER
Real-time site risk scores, weather overlays, crew deployment, inspection tracking, and daily standup reports — all in one dashboard tailored to your role.

PROJECT & CONTRACT MANAGEMENT
Track active projects with progress bars, budgets, and team assignments. Manage your bid pipeline with scoring, watchers, and live feed status. Full Supabase cloud sync.

FIELD OPERATIONS
Daily field reports, GPS-tracked crew timecards with overtime calculation, equipment asset tracking with service schedules, and permit management with expiration alerts.

OPERATIONS SUITE
12 specialized panels: Command Center, Change Orders, Safety Incidents, Material Deliveries, Punch List, Subcontractor Scorecards, Daily Costs, Submittals, Project Accounts, Executive Portfolio, RFI Tracker, and Budget Burn Rate.

FINANCIAL TOOLS
AIA G702/G703 pay application generation, lien waiver management, cash flow forecasting, expense tracking with 12 IRS categories, quarterly tax estimates, and 1099 subcontractor tracking.

EQUIPMENT RENTALS
Search 97+ items across 20 categories from United Rentals, DOZR, Toolsy, Rent My Equipment, Sunbelt, and Herc Rentals. AI equipment recommender, price alerts, bundle builder, operator marketplace, and provider account linking.

AI-POWERED INTELLIGENCE
Angelic AI assistant powered by Claude with 18 MCP tools for real-time data access. Ask about site status, crew deployment, budgets, equipment, and more — get answers backed by live project data.

WEALTH INTELLIGENCE
Money Lens, Psychology Decoder, Power Thinking Framework, Leverage System, and High Income Opportunity Filter — five tools for building construction wealth.

COMPLIANCE & SAFETY
OSHA toolbox talks with sign-off tracking, certified payroll (WH-347), environmental compliance (SWPPP), and training certification management with expiration alerts.

SECURITY
Face ID/Touch ID lock, Keychain-encrypted API keys, two-factor authentication, trusted device management, and credential vault.
